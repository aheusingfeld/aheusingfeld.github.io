Short Biography

I was born on Feb 24th, 1981 in Hilden, Germany. Some time ago I was a band member of Freiwelt (formerly known as FREI). Today I'm married, got 3 wonderful kids and work as a Senior Consultant for software development at innoQ Deutschland GmbH as senior consultant for software development. You can find more details on my preliminary projects on the "Über mich"-Page in my Xing-Profile (German). My focus is clearly software development with Java (TM) and especially "Spring Framework" and Spring Projects.

As a side job I'm working as a freelance IT consultant. Under the name of First Point IT Solutions me and a few other freelancers are building individual web applications for small and medium sized companies.

You can find more information about myself on the various profiles in the web which are linked on this page.

What means 'goldstift'?

'goldstift' is a nickname I got from one of my boss when I was a trainee. It is actually a combined word from the two words "gold" (referencing the valuable metal) and "stift" which is a colloquial German word for "trainee". Back in the days all trainees at our company were called "Stift". My boss and my colleagues said they really valued my work and to exoress this they started calling me "goldstift".

I felt a little proud about this and some time later started using this nickname myself - and I still do until today.
